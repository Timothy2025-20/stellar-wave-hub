# Payabroad - Stellar Wave Project Research

## Project Overview
Payabroad is a payment solution built on the Stellar network that enables street vendors, small shop owners, and local businesses in tourist destinations to accept cross-border payments from international travelers using stablecoins. The project participated in the Stellar Community Fund Growth Hack Cohort 1 launched in January 2026.

## Problem Statement
International travelers face numerous challenges when making payments abroad: high currency exchange fees (typically 3-5%), unfavorable exchange rates, limited access to local payment methods, and security concerns with carrying cash. For vendors, accepting foreign currency involves additional exchange costs and risks. Payabroad solves both sides of this problem.

## Solution & Technology
Payabroad leverages the Stellar network's fast settlement (3-5 seconds) and low transaction fees ($0.00001 per transaction). Key features include:

- **QR Code System**: Vendors generate QR codes for their wallet addresses
- **Instant Settlement**: Payments settle in USDC on Stellar within seconds
- **No Banking Required**: Works without traditional banking infrastructure
- **Traveler-Friendly**: Tourists pay with stablecoins from any wallet

## Stellar Integration
The project uses Stellar's built-in decentralized exchange and USDC (Circle's stablecoin) for stable settlements. This eliminates volatility risk while maintaining the speed and low cost of Stellar transactions.

Payabroad demonstrates a real-world use case for Stellar in the travel and tourism sector. It enables:
- Financial access for unbanked vendors
- Reduced payment friction for travelers
